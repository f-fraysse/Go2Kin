# 4. Connecting the GoPros

Always connect and disconnect in this order — it keeps USB detection and camera control
reliable.

## To connect (each camera)

1. Insert a (charged) **battery**.
2. Power the camera **on**.
3. Connect the **USB cable** to the PC.

Then, in the GUI, use the per-camera **Connect** buttons in the
[bottom bar](../gui/bottom-bar.md). The indicator turns green when connected, and a
standard set of settings is applied automatically (Linear lens, Hypersmooth off, etc.).

## To disconnect (reverse order)

1. **Disconnect** in the GUI.
2. Unplug the **USB cable**.
3. Power the camera **off**.
